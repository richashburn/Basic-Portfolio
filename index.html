About Me

I'm Rich Ashburn. My family moved to Richmond in 2018 and we love it. In Denver, my wife and I owned a hair salon for 16 years and I taught elementary school. We decided it was time for a little change in our lives so we sold the business and moved to Richmond. We couldn't be happier with our decision! Riding the wave of change, I decided to pivot away from teaching and try something I was interested in, web development. After telling kids for years to become life long learners - I decided to take my own advice.

In addition to web development, I'm learning about the hiking paths along the James River, trying to teach myself the banjo and debating if my daughter's quest to become a circus performer is "college fund worthy".
